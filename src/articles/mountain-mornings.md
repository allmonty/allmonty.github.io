## The Golden Hour

Early light changes everything. These shots were taken between 6 and 8 AM, when the world is still quiet and the sun hits at angles that make ordinary scenes feel extraordinary.

[![Mountain valley with morning mist](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1506905925346-21bda4d32df4?auto=format&fit=crop&w=1200&q=80)](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1506905925346-21bda4d32df4?auto=format&fit=crop&w=2400&q=90)

## By the Water

Lakeside towns at dawn have a stillness you won't find later in the day. The reflections are perfect mirrors, and there's usually no one around but locals with coffee.

[![Alpine lake at sunrise](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1506905925346-21bda4d32df4?auto=format&fit=crop&w=1200&q=80)](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1506905925346-21bda4d32df4?auto=format&fit=crop&w=2400&q=90)
